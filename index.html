Knrukshetra University Kurukshetra is one of the best platforms for the budding artists of Haryana State. In the domain of Haryanvi Culture, Ratnawali gives the energy to folk artists of our state and spread the different color of folk art like folk dance, folk music, Folk theater, folk literature and folk handicraft. This year, the "Ratnawali" Haryana Day State Level Festival "MAHAKUMBHA" of Haryanavi Culture is being organized from 26th to 29th October, 2018 in the University Campus vigorously. This festival is a theme festival of Haryana State. The Students of all the Colleges and Universities of Haryana State participate in this festival. Last year, around 3500 students participated in this Festival. The participation in this festival is increasing day by day in manifolds. Professional Groups like, Jangam Jogi, Sapera Party, Bansali Party, Sarangi Party, Deru Party are also invited to give their performances just to show the real values of glimpses of Haryana's rituals to the new generation to follow it. The Department has initiated to revive an extinct Dance Form of "Haryanvi Loor Dance". In addition to providing a plateform to the performers in this event documentation of the festival is also being due to preserve the art forms and help in their prosperity. This rule book is a step in this direction. I hope this rule book will serve as a guideline to the performers in this cultural festival.
